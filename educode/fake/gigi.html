Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Do I need any prior coding experience to join EduCode?

No prior coding experience is required. Our platform is designed for absolute beginners. We start with the fundamentals and gradually progress to more advanced topics. Our 7-month and 1-year paths are specifically designed for beginners.

How much time do I need to commit daily?

We recommend dedicating 2-3 hours per day for consistent progress. Our daily roadmaps are designed to be completed within this timeframe, making it manageable even for working professionals or students.

What kind of support can I expect?

You'll have access to our peer support forums, code buddy system, doubt discussion rooms, and weekly live mentoring sessions. Our instructors are also available to help you through challenging concepts.

Is the certificate recognized by employers?

Yes, our certificates are recognized by many employers in the tech industry. The certificate validates your skills and the portfolio projects you've completed during the program, which is often more important than the certificate itself.

Can I switch between different tech stacks?

Yes, you can switch between different tech stacks at any time. While we recommend sticking with one stack to build depth, we understand that career goals may change. Your progress will be saved, and you can continue from where you left off.
